About Me:

I am a self-motivated individual who loves to spread positivity, laughs, and smiles wherever I go. Hard work and communication are two things I highly value. I grew up doing highly competitive gymnastics, where I attended an olympic training center. Through this experience, I am able to push myself way beyond my comfort zone and thrive under pressure. Through childhood adversitisies, I am able to utilize my internal strength to push forward and to show compassion and empathy to all. I am a great listener who loves to help people. I am passionate about Psychology, as mental health is something that is so easily overlooked in life, yet so very important. Through my knowledge in hard work, psychology, empathy, and compassion, I believe I would make an exemplary Special Events Director for the 2020-2021 school year in the Associated Students Program Board at the University of California, Riverside.

I am currently an intern for the Associated Students Program Board on campus, where I help organize, plan, and execute student-run events. These events range from concerts, to throwback nights, to bonfires, to comedy shows, to free movie premieres. I am in the Special Events Division, where myself and four other interns work under our two directors and their two advisors. Within this position, we have learned to send various inquires, day of event duty sheets, and we also have learned how to be a leader. Through this program, I have found a passion for making people happy and for making people's various dreams come true.

I am also currently an employee at the Student Recreation Center in the Youth Programs sector. My position includes watching over children, ranging from 18 months old to 16 years of age. I work alongside seven other employees, under one student manager, and under one advisor. We are able to help the children with their homework, play with them, and help them socialize with other children. Overall, I tend to the parents comfort levels of what their children do, and I ensure the safety of all of the children. I am a Mandatory Reporter, AED/First Aid/CPR Certified, and trained in Cyber Security and Sexual Harassment Awareness.

I am looking to further my professionalism in the workplace. I want to meet more people, network, and find even more opportuinities to indulge myself in. I really love being surrounded by my peers and enjoying the campus culture. I hope to find various organizations to join, or at least expereience.
